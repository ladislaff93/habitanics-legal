Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

Habitanics is a habit-tracking app with farming gamification. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Storage

All your personal data — habits, todos, garden progress, inventory, and game state — is stored locally on your device only. We do not have servers that store your data. We cannot access, view, or recover your data.

Analytics & Feedback

We use TelemetryDeck to collect anonymous, non-personal usage analytics. This helps us understand how the app is used and improve it. TelemetryDeck does not collect IP addresses, device IDs, or any personally identifiable information. See TelemetryDeck's Privacy Policy.

We use WishKit to allow users to submit feedback and feature requests. Feedback is submitted voluntarily and may be visible to other users. See WishKit's Privacy Policy.

No Account Required

Habitanics does not require registration, login, email, or any personal information to use.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling the app permanently deletes all your data.

Third-Party Services

  • TelemetryDeck (anonymous analytics)
  • WishKit (user feedback and feature requests)
  • Apple (App Store, in-app purchases)

Contact

If you have questions, reach us at support@habitanics.app.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
